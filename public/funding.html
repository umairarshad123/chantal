Funding FAQs

Do I need good personal credit to get funded? +

Strong personal credit helps, but we also build your business credit profile — and can work on improving your personal credit alongside it for the best results.

How much funding can I qualify for? +

It depends on your credit, revenue, and structure. In your free assessment we'll give you a realistic range and a plan to increase it.

How long does it take? +

Some clients are funded quickly; others need a few weeks to become fundable first. We give you a clear timeline up front.

Can you help with mortgage readiness too? +

Yes. We position you for homeownership with credit, income documentation, and debt strategies tailored to lender requirements.
